🔬 Explore Mitosis Phases

🔎 Click on the phases to learn more about it!
🎬 Prophase: Chromosomes condense, spindle fibers form, and the nuclear envelope breaks down.
📏 Metaphase: Chromosomes align at the cell’s equator—Chromie’s runway moment!
💔 Anaphase: Sister chromatids are pulled apart to opposite poles. A dramatic cellular split!
🎭 Telophase: Nuclear membranes reform, chromosomes de-condense, and the cell prepares to divide.
